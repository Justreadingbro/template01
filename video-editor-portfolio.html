Capabilities

What I bring to the edit

I specialise in narrative-first editing — finding the emotional spine of a cut before worrying about pace or rhythm. From assembly to picture lock, I work closely with directors and brands to protect the story through every revision.

My colour work ranges from naturalistic doc grades to highly stylised LUT-based looks for branded content. Motion graphics and title design are built in-house, keeping the creative loop tight and fast.

How I Work

The process

Phase 01
Brief & Discovery
A focused call to understand your story, your audience, and what success looks like before a single frame is cut.
Phase 02
Rough Assembly
All selects organised. A first cut that finds the emotional arc — imperfect on purpose, open to change.
Phase 03
Revision Loop
Two rounds of structured feedback via Frame.io. Every comment addressed, every change tracked and explained.
Phase 04
Delivery
Picture lock, audio polish, colour grade, and exports in every format you need — on time, with full project handover.
